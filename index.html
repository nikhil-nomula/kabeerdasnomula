About

A lifetime of bringing people together

Nomula Kabeer Das has spent his life in the service of our community. As President of Jinkala Bavi (JB) Colony, he believed that development should never wait for politics. He sat down with leaders of every party, BJP, Congress, and TRS alike, and worked with all of them so that the people of the colony would get what they deserve.

For decades he served as an All-India union leader for BSNL, representing workers across the country, standing up for their rights, and learning how to organise, negotiate, and deliver results. Now retired from service, he is ready to give that same energy and experience fully to our ward.

His strength has always been simple: he listens to everyone, he keeps no enemies, and he gets the work done. That is the kind of leader a ward needs as its Councillor.
